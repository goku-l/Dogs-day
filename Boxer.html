Details of Boxer

They are patient and spirited with children, but also protective, making them a popular choice for families. They are active, strong dogs that require adequate exercise to prevent boredom-associated behaviors such as chewing, digging, or licking. Running, jogging, playing fetch, mild wrestling, tug of war, and swimming are all great ways to exercise a boxer.

Physical characteristics

  • Medium sized dogs
  • Short haired
  • Strong heavy head
  • Powerful jaws

Personality

  • Active
  • Intelligent
  • obedient
  • Loyal to his family and mingles well with children
  • Easy to train

Origin : Germany
Life span : 10 to 12 years
Height : Male - 23 to 25 inches
              Female - 21.5 to 23.5 inches
Weight : Male - 65 to 70 lbs
              Female - 55 to 60 lbs
