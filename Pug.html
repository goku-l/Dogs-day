Details of Pug

Pugs are known for being sociable and gentle companion dogs. The pug gets its name from the Latin word that means ‘clenched fist’, because of the flat nose. With their comical face and hilarious personalities, pugs are the jesters of the canine world. They do very well with children and other animals alike, especially if socialized from a young age

Physical characteristics

  • Flat face
  • short nose
  • A black facial mark
  • short body

Personality

  • Don't need much exercise
  • Love playing and jovial in nature
  • Funny
  • Don't like to work out
  • Loves to stay close with the owner
  • Difficult to train

Origin : China
Life span : 12 to 15 years
Height : 10 to 14 inches
Weight : 14 to 18 lbs
